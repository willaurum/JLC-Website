About the Club

The Liberty University Japanese Language Club is a group of students dedicated to becoming fluent in Japanese through practice, conversation, and cultural exploration.

What is this club?

We are a welcoming community of students who practice Japanese together and explore Japanese culture in a relaxed, supportive setting.

Great for: new learners, returning students, and anyone who loves language and culture.

Why Join

A comfortable, friendly space to learn, practice, and connect.

Consistent Practice

Weekly meetings help you build confidence and keep learning steady.

Cultural Exploration

Discover traditions, media, and everyday life in Japan with other students.

Community

Make friends who share your interest in Japanese language and culture.
