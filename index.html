About the Club

Our mission is to prepare students for mission work in Japan through education on the culture and the language of the region. We aim to teach interested/eager students the Japanese language, and provide for them the necessary materials and practice to effectively learn the language.

FAQ

Do I need experience?

No. Beginners and advanced speakers are welcome.

What should I bring?

A notebook and pencil is great! A laptop can be useful too.

How will I learn?

Our club is built like a classroom dynamic. Each week you will go to the class of your skill level and you will go over lessons planned by our japanese teachers.

What if I already know Japanese?

We have classes for all skill levels including classes for people who are fluent. Additionally if you would like to teach a class we offer CSER credits for that as well!
