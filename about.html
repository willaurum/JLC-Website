Club Overview

The club is open to students at any level and gives everyone a place to keep practicing.

We meet weekly to help students grow in their understanding of Japanese language and culture. The club is designed to be a place where students can practice consistently, ask questions, and keep improving over time.

Our Focus

Practice, conversation, and cultural exploration.

Mission Details

Our goal is to support students who want to learn Japanese and prepare for future opportunities to serve, teach, and engage with Japanese culture in meaningful ways.

Club Values

The club should feel approachable, useful, and easy to return to.

Accessible

Easy for new members to join in right away.

Consistent

Regular meetings help build confidence over time.

Curiosity

Language and culture belong together.

Mission Minded

We equip students with Japanese language skills that can support future missions work in Japan for Christ.
